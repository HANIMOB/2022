Submission Guidelines

Submissions from researchers in the form of short papers (4-6 pages) are welcomed, on topics including:

  • availability and downscaling of human mobility data for animal movement analysis
  • integration of human mobility in animal movement analysis
  • integration of ecological concepts in human mobility analysis
  • geospatial and mobility data integration
  • use cases in human mobility and/or animal movement analysis
  • software platforms and visualization techniques for mobility data analysis

All submissions and reviews will be handled electronically through the EasyChair submission site: http://easychair.org/.
Submitted papers must be uploaded in PDF format and formatted according to the standard double-column ACM SIG proceedings guidelines that also apply to the ACM SIGSPATIAL conference submissions. Additional information about formatting and style files are available online .
